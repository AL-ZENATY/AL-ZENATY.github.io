About MeOver mijعني

Who I amWie ik benمن أنا

I am Abdulla Zenaty, a 23-year-old MBO-4 Software Developer student with a strong interest in frontend development, UI/UX design and web applications. I was born in Syria and moved to the Netherlands in 2015, which taught me to adapt, keep learning and work hard toward my goals. I enjoy building interfaces that are not only functional, but also visually clean, smooth and easy to use. My interest in design and photography helps me look at websites with an eye for layout, spacing, color, atmosphere and detail.

Ik ben Abdulla Zenaty, 23 jaar oud en MBO-4 Software Developer student. Mijn grootste interesse ligt bij frontend development, UI/UX design en webapplicaties. Ik ben geboren in Syrië en ben in 2015 naar Nederland gekomen. Daardoor heb ik geleerd om mij aan te passen, door te blijven leren en serieus aan mijn doelen te werken.
